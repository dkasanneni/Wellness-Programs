FAQ

Is this medical advice?

No. This site is for general education. For diagnosis or treatment, consult a licensed clinician.

Do I need special equipment?

A mat, a stable chair, and a light resistance band are enough to start.

How fast will I see results?

Consistency matters most. Many people feel improvements in comfort and confidence in a few weeks.
